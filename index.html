for of loop

        
The for...of loop iterates over iterable objects (including arrays, strings, maps, sets, etc.), returning the values of the elements.


        let fruits = ["Apple", "Banana", "Cherry"];

        
        for (let fruit of fruits) {
            console.log(fruit);
        }
